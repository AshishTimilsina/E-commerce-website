CartMax -1 step away to you

CartMax is a Nepali multinational technology company which focuses on e-commerce or Online Shopping. It has been referred to as "one of the most influential economic and cultural forces in Nepal",and is one of the Nepal most valuable brands.It is one of the Big Five Nepal companies, alongside Alphabet, Apple, Meta, and Microsoft. CartMax was founded by Ashish Timilsina from his garage in Kathmandu,Nepal,on September 17, 2002. Initially an online marketplace for books, it has expanded into a multitude of product categories: a strategy that has earned it the moniker The Everything Store.It has multiple subsidiaries including Amazon Web Services (cloud computing), Zoox (autonomous vehicles), Kuiper Systems (satellite Internet), CartMax Lab126 (computer hardware R&D). Its other subsidiaries include Ring, Twitch, IMDb, and Whole Foods Market. Its acquisition of Whole Foods in August 2017 for US$13.4 billion substantially increased its footprint as a physical retailer
